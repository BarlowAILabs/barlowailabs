Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11 November 2025

Barlow AI Labs ("Barlow AI", "we", "us", or "our") respects the privacy of our users ("you" or "your"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you visit our website (Barlow AI Labs) and use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information about you:

  • Personal Information: This includes information you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, email address, and any message you send through our contact form.
  • Usage and Technical Information: This includes information collected automatically when you use the Website, such as your IP address, device type, browsing history, and how you interact with our site. We collect this via cookies and analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our Website.
  • To respond to your inquiries and requests, such as when you fill out our contact form.
  • To improve and personalize your experience on the Website.
  • To send you an auto-reply email after you contact us.
  • To prevent spam and secure our Website (e.g., via reCAPTCHA).
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

Third-Party Services

Our website is built using Google Cloud services, including Firebase and Google Analytics. By using our site, you acknowledge that your information may be processed by these services.

  • Firebase (Google Cloud): Our website hosting, backend functions (contact form), and email queuing (Trigger Email Extension) are powered by Google's Firebase platform.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors engage with our site. This service collects anonymous data to help us improve user experience.
  • Google reCAPTCHA: We use reCAPTCHA on our contact form to prevent spam. This service checks if you are a human and is subject to the Google Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with Google as described in the "Third-Party Services" section to operate our website. We may also disclose your information if we are required to do so by law.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no website or internet transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Your Choices

You can use browser settings to block or delete cookies and opt-out of Google Analytics. You can also choose not to submit our contact form if you do not wish to provide your personal information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the Website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email at tevin@barlowailabs.com.
